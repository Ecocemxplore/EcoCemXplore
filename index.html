About EcoCemXplore

EcoCemXplore is a Marie Skłodowska-Curie Postdoctoral Fellowship project focused on developing next-generation low-carbon cementitious materials.

The project aims to reduce CO₂ emissions in the construction sector through innovative material design and advanced characterization.
